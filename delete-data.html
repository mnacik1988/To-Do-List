Data Deletion — Mynado

App: Mynado · Developer: Aleksandr (mnacik1988@gmail.com) · updated 8 July 2026

This page explains how to delete your data from the Mynado app (package app.mynado.twa).

How to delete your data

  1. Open Mynado on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear) in the top-right corner.
  3. Scroll down and tap “Clear all data”.
  4. Confirm. This permanently deletes all your tasks stored on the device and cancels any reminders stored on our server.

You can also request deletion by email at mnacik1988@gmail.com — we will process the request within 30 days.

What data is deleted

  • Tasks, dates, settings — stored only on your device; removed immediately by “Clear all data”.
  • Reminders (task text + time) — stored on our server only while a reminder is active; removed by “Clear all data” and automatically deleted after the reminder fires.
  • Anonymous device identifier — used only to deliver push notifications; removed when you clear data or disable reminders.

What data is retained

Mynado does not require registration and does not store your name, email, password or payment data. After deletion, no personal data is retained on our servers. We keep no analytics or advertising profiles.

Удаление данных (RU)

Чтобы удалить свои данные из приложения Mynado: откройте приложение → значок «Настройки» (шестерёнка) вверху справа → «Очистить все данные» → подтвердите. Это навсегда удалит все задачи с устройства и отменит напоминания на сервере. Также можно запросить удаление письмом на mnacik1988@gmail.com (обработаем в течение 30 дней). Приложение не требует регистрации и не хранит имя, email, пароль или платёжные данные.
